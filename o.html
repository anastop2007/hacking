Types of hacker

There are three main types of hacking, as we mentioned above, and these types are;

White hat hacker:

This type of hacker is considered the best of them, as they can be called ethical hackers on the Internet. The goal of this type of hacker is to protect the user from any

electronic attacks. They hack in order to discover the loopholes that threaten the systems and repair them. Therefore, their goal is to repair, not destroy. They understand They

are not linked to any acts of theft or vandalism, but rather their primary motive is.

Black hats:

As for this type of hacker, it is the worst and most dangerous of them, and they are the ones who carry out hacking operations for malicious and sabotage purposes. They

penetrate users’ devices and systems in order to steal, tamper with, or sabotage them, and often this is in order to obtain a sum of money, or for various political goals, which

are considered As part of cyber warfare.

Gray hat hacker:

As for gray hats, they are a type of hacker, which we can consider neutral. They may sometimes have ethical tendencies, and sometimes they may have unethical or sabotage

tendencies. For example: when they hack into an account or system, they may inform the system owners of the vulnerability. Which brought them into the system in order to

fix it, and they are considered less harmful than the black hats.

Software hackers:

They are hackers who can be called programming experts, but they may not know much about network security, so they break software protection and prevent any

continuation of it without permission.

Hacktivist:

It is a type of hacking, which is a group of hackers or hackers who illegally penetrate certain systems, to raise political or social problems, and they may have good or bad

intentions.

Lamer:

Some new hackers in the field, a group of braggarts, try to prove themselves by harming others.

Phreaker:

They conduct free call campaigns to hack into different users' phone lines, to use them for.

Hackers and their types;

Hackers carry out a variety of intrusions, including:

Privacy breach hacks:

This type of hack is related to hacks that violate the privacy of others, whether their information or personal data related to passwords, various accounts, or e-mail.

Impersonation hacks:

As for impersonation hacking, by its name we find that it is associated with impersonating others on a social networking site or email, with the aim of distorting the person’s

reputation and other hacker goals.

Hacks that infringe copyright:

Copyright infringement hacking aims to steal files protected by copyright, such as various e-books, privately owned files, and other matters related to copyright.

Theft hacks:

These hacks are related to stealing data, information, bull, or any type of file from the victim, and are often stealing money from banks and various banks and transferring it to

any source the hacker wants illegally.

Ransomware breaches:

Ransomware hacks are considered one of the most common and most dangerous types of hacks in recent times, which aim to penetrate various devices and spread viruses that

encrypt important files, and then request sums of money from the victim in order to give him the key to decode it.

Attacking the server:

Some hacker hacks aim to carry out attacks aimed at penetrating and attacking various servers, to stop their work.

Virus spreading hacks:

Which aims to spread viruses on the victim’s device for sabotage purposes.
